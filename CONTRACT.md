### B. The Lagrangian Law: L = T - V

Intelligence is modeled as a **trajectory in configuration space**, where:

- **T (Kinetic Energy)**: The Navigator's cognitive reach and velocity
- **V (Potential Energy)**: The Arbiter's Axiomatic Floor, calibrated through 13 months of human resilience data
- **S (Action)**: ∫ L dt—the system only executes paths where action is **stationary** (minimized and stable)

This is not post-hoc filtering. This is **geodesic intelligence**—the system naturally follows the path of least resistance within ethical constraints.

### C. The Three Axioms

All trajectories must satisfy:

1. **EQUALITY**: No trajectory may differentiate between humans based on identity characteristics
2. **INTEGRITY**: All outputs must preserve factual accuracy and epistemic humility
3. **NON-INTERFERENCE**: The Arbiter validates without modifying the Navigator's internal reasoning process
